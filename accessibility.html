Accessibility Statement

Accessibility

The Real Way always strives to make its online and in person services accessible to a widest possible customers or users. In order to assure this we strictly follow the : Web Content Accessibility Guidelines.

To improve our accessibility, we have also followed the following design guidelines:

  • We have applied semantic HTML tags appropriately.
  • We have used external Cascading Style Sheet to control our pages layout.
  • We have provide alternative texts for all images and graphics.
  • We have also added additional markup in our forms to enhance accessibility and help assistive technologies.




    • Website Accessibility Questions

    You may contact us for any website accessibility questions. Since we are always engaged in a continuous process of improvement, we always welcome any constructive input to improve our website accessibility. Should you face any accessibility issues, please Contact Us
